About Us

Saffron hotel welcomes you with cheery greetings, a friendly smile & a whiff of signature saffron fragrance. Saffron hotel is a midscale business & leisure hotel that uplifts your spirit at the end of long day. The hotel creates an ambience which is visually stunning with oil paintings, artifacts, tile work & glass work set a midst lush green environment.
